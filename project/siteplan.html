Aspindale SkillSwap

Site Plan

Site Name

Aspindale SkillSwap - A peer-to-peer bartering platform for skill exchanges in the Aspindale community.

I chose this name because it clearly indicates both the location (Aspindale) and purpose (skill swapping) of the platform. The alliteration makes it memorable.

Site Purpose

Aspindale SkillSwap will provide a platform where community members can:

  • Offer their skills/services to others
  • Request needed services from the community
  • Connect with compatible trade partners
  • Schedule skill exchange sessions
  • Build trust through a rating system

The site aims to foster community connections while providing economic benefits through non-monetary exchanges.

Scenarios

Target audience: Budget-conscious individuals, students, retirees, and anyone looking to share skills without monetary exchange.

  1. "I'm a web developer looking to learn guitar. Are there any guitar teachers in Aspindale who need website help?"
  2. "How can I verify that someone is reliable before agreeing to a skill swap?"
  3. "What's the best way to schedule multiple tutoring sessions for math in exchange for gardening help?"
